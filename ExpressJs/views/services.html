Ski-Touring - 3 days of skiing

Visit Senja for those magical skiing moments. Ski from summit to sea, every meter of altitude is utilized. Senja has mountains that are suitable for everyone, regardless of level. Certified, local knowledgable guides, we adjust the tours according to expectations and level of the group.

Polar Light snowmobile safari

This tour is conducted by Camp Tamok & Ice Domes. Experience the magical evening landscape of Camp Tamok whilst driving a snowmobile through snowy paths. If you are really lucky the Northern Lights will be dancing above you all evening! Even though this tour is not focused on hunting the green lights, but it gives a great chance to be a part of the Aurora performance in the night sky.
